Azure AI provides a range of **cognitive services and AI tools** to build intelligent applications:

- **Cognitive Services:** Vision, Speech, Language APIs  
- **Azure OpenAI:** LLMs for custom AI applications  
- **Machine Learning Service:** Train, deploy, and manage ML models
